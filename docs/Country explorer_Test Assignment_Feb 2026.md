**О компании**

Getplace — платформа competitive intelligence для рынка доставки еды. Мы помогаем крупным ресторанным сетям и агрегаторам доставки принимать решения на основе данных: доли рынка, зоны доставки, видимость в приложении, цены, отзывы и многое другое.

Наши клиенты — крупные ресторанные сети. Для них важно понимать, что происходит с конкурентами в конкретной стране: где они присутствуют, где открываются и закрываются, как выглядят в сравнении с другими брендами.

**\[English\] Test Assignment: Country Explorer**

**About the company**

Getplace is a competitive intelligence platform for the food delivery market. We help large restaurant chains and delivery platforms make data-driven decisions: market share, delivery zones, in-app visibility, pricing, reviews and much more.

**Task context**

Our clients are large restaurant chains. It's important for them to understand what's happening with their competitors in a specific country: where they operate, where they're opening and closing locations, how they compare to other brands.

We want to build a feature called **Country Explorer** that will allow our clients to explore the presence of key restaurant brands in a country and draw conclusions about the competitive landscape.

This is not an abstract exercise. Country Explorer is a real feature we're planning to launch soon. The person who does well on this assignment will be the one shipping it.

**Data**

Along with this assignment you'll receive real data on England:

* Region polygons  
* Restaurant brand locations with coordinates and attributes

Data: [Here](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1uwqJtVNY2YJ-qDYNMlNbHZGi51QUNYsV?usp=drive_link)

Use this data as your foundation. Feel free to supplement it with your own assumptions about what other data would be useful (regional/city population, location opening dates, cuisine categories, etc.).

**What you need to do**

1. **Product concept** Describe the feature: who it's for, what problem it solves, key use cases. Define what goes into the MVP and what comes in later iterations.  
2. **Interactive prototype** Build a clickable prototype. Preferred tool is Lovable. We want to see how you think about UX and data visualization.  
3. **Roadmap** Think through a short-term roadmap for the feature. What do we build first, what comes next, and why in that order.  
4. **Monetization** Describe the value you see in this feature for our business. How can we use it.  
5. **Development documentation** Describe the feature so that a developer can start working without additional questions. Structure, logic, element behavior, data handling.  
6. **Feature landing page** Think through how to sell this feature to a client: page structure, key messages, CTA. Format is up to you.

One document (Notion, Google Docs or PDF) with all sections \+ a link to the prototype.

**What we evaluate**

* Product thinking: how you prioritize, what you focus on
